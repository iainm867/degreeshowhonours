Welcome to the Computing Degree Show 2019


It is our very great pleasure to welcome you to the 2019 Computing Degree Show.
Whether you are family, friends, School members, visitors from elsewhere in the University, from business or the community, there is so much gathered here for you to see and admire.

The Computing Degree Show 2019 celebrates the creative talent of nearly 65 students about to complete the final year of their undergraduate courses, with exhibits ranging from computer games to support for people with learning disabilities or with colour vision deficiency, to business-focused applications and artificial intelligence tools.

Software engineers, games developers, computer programmers, tech entrepreneurs and other digital pioneers from the University of Dundee are showing their innovative and thought-provoking work.
