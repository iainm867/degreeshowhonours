About my Project

The aim of my project is:
Each year, the Computing Degree Show allows Honours project students to show their projects to
the staff and students in Computing and to the wider university community, family, friends etc.
In addition to the show itself we have a website describing each project.The website I had
in mind would follow along suit with the current colour scheme and feel of the University of Dundee
Website which would show friends, fellow students and employers easy to ingest information showcasing
what each student had done.
